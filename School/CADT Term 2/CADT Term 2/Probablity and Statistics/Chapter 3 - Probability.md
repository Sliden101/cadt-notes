# Conditional Probability

Probabilty of B given A

$$P(B|A)=\frac{P(B\cap{A})}{P(A)}$$
Probability of A given B
$$P(A|B)=\frac{P(B\cap{A})}{P(B)}$$

$$P(\neg{A}|B)=\frac{P(B\cap{\neg{A}})}{P(B)}$$
$$P(\neg{B}|A)=\frac{P(\neg{B}\cap{A})}{P(A)}$$
